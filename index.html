Biography

Welcome to my personal webpage!

I am a tenure track Assistant Professor of Economics at CY Cergy Paris Université and a researcher at Thema. My research interests are in the fields of Econometrics (Theory and Application), Social Interactions, Networks, and Health Economics.

I received my PhD in Economics from Université Laval in 2021.
